## Writing Style Guidelines

### Tone Transformation

**Old Tone** (Student Project):
- Friendly and approachable
- Mentions specific libraries
- Focus on helping families
- Technical implementation details
- Educational explanations

**New Tone** (Enterprise Product):
- Professional and authoritative
- Focus on capabilities, not tools
- Emphasize business value
- Strategic positioning
- Industry leadership

### Example Transformations

#### Example 1: Feature Description

**Before**:
> "Using OpenCV and face_recognition libraries, our system extracts facial encodings and performs similarity matching with configurable confidence thresholds."

**After**:
> "Advanced deep learning models deliver precision identity matching with adaptive confidence scoring and multi-view profile recognition capabilities."

#### Example 2: Value Proposition

**Before**:
> "Built with Flask and powered by advanced computer vision algorithms, our platform processes photos and videos using state-of-the-art facial recognition technology."

**After**:
> "Our platform leverages cutting-edge neural networks and advanced pattern recognition to process visual data at scale, delivering actionable intelligence to organizations worldwide."

#### Example 3: Call-to-Action

**Before**:
> "Ready to Get Started? Our platform is designed to be user-friendly while providing powerful AI capabilities."

**After**:
> "Transform Your Identity Intelligence. Join leading organizations worldwide leveraging VisionPulse AI for mission-critical operations."
